**Goals**: To show that direct translation doesn’t work.. Go from Japanese to English

**Materials**: 


* [Worksheet of Movie Titles](../worksheets/movie-worksheet.md)
* Pictures of the Movies (Optional)

**Preparation:**

Don’t really need to do anything other than print and bring the worksheet (1 per student).

**Procedure:**



1. If you want to warm up the class you can tell them the names of some Ghibli movies (See below)  in English and see if they can guess the name in Japanese. (Maybe do Wasei-Warmup if you get the chance)
2. Hand out the worksheets. Have students go over it and talk with the people around them if they please.
3. Correct the answers.
